El dia de los Blogs.

This blog will be dedicated to all things paranormal, from the well known
"Mothman" or "La Llorona" to tales i've have heard from friends to
stories I heard from my grandmother as a child, and of course my own
personal experiences. I find it curious how this subject frigthens people
but also intrigues them, and you can include me in that group. I remember
my brother and I as children begging my abuela to tell us ghost stories
but then at night having to sleep with the lights on, because as soon as the
lights went off, all those stories came to life or death, I guess depending on
how you looked at it. But every time we saw my grandmother, there we would go
again pestering her until she broke down and told us a story

So, this is the first of many posts to come. I hope it will entertain some,
scare others, and maybe remind some of things they may have heard or seen
but had forgotten or maybe wanted to forget. So here we go, my abuela

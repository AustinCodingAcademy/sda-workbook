Arctic Wolf Facts:

The Arctic Wolf is awesome because it can survive in the coldest of climates Arctic wolves are smaller than grey wolves, They also have smaller ears and shorter muzzles to retain body heat. The Arctic Wolf is about 40"-70" in length including tail, they can weight anywhere from 99-154lbs. The Artic Wolf has diet of mainly muskox, Artic hares and caribou, as the permafrost (permanently frozen ground) prevents the Arctic wolf from digging a den, they typically live in rocky outcrops or caves. Each year the mother wolf gives birth to two or three pups, the Artic wolf can be found mainly in the Arctic regions of North Amercia and Greenland.

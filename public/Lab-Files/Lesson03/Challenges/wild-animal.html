The coolest creature

The Gorilla is awesome because strength and intelligence.

Even though gorillas are physically large and look intimidating, they are shy animals that live in small
groups of 6 to 12, with one dominate male called a “silverback” that leads the group. Silverbacks on average
are about five and half feet tall and weigh about 500 lbs. with an estimated strength of 20 average men. The
silverback is very protective of his family and will not hesitate to defend them from a threat. Due to this
reason, they are usually the most aggressive of he group but some call it just a show of aggression because
they make several attempts to scare off anyone that intrudes into the area, they take these steps in an
effort to avoid confrontation. They pound their chest, break small trees and charge the intruders, most of
the time this will be enough to make anyone run away, but don’t think they are afraid of confrontation, if
it comes to it, a silverback will give its life protecting his family.

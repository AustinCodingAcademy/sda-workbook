The coolest creature

The Lion is awesome because it is one of the strongest and most powerful of all the wild creatures. Lions are known to project a regal and majestic quality when hunting and caring for their young. They are native to the sub-Saharan African Desert and are the the only large cats to travel in groups called "Prides." Lions are known to be very affectionate towards one another and also very expressive through their physical movements and behaviors. Lions are extaordinary animals that symbolize strength and beauty and I will always admire them.
